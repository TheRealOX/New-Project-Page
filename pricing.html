Methodology


We use the 11 features in our database to predict the price of a used car. All textual features have been encoded into distinct labels. After tuning and experimenting with over 15 ML models, we have settled on Random Forest Regressor as it provided the lowest RMSE (although none of the models were able to produce a small RMSE possibly due to the large number of encoding of column values). 

Our web app offers features such as prediction, data insertion, model training and uploading, etc..
