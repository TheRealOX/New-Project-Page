Cleaning



The dataset was messy, with many columns and row having missing values and/or extra information not needed. As a result, the pre-processing was extensive as we cleaned out irrelevant columns (such as entry ID, region URL, etc.) and dropped rows with too many empty cells. Moreover, we conducted heavy textual analysis and cleaning on features such as manufacturer and model to ensure the consistency in the texts.

The resulting dataset has over 320k entries with 12 important features (including price).
