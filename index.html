AN EXPLORATION ON USED CARS DATA


By Hao Niu, Dading Shi


ABOUT THE PROJECT


We chose to look at data for used cars because of the decrease in production of new cars due to the shortage in semiconductors caused by the global COVID-19 pandemic. As a result, the people who do not wish to pay a premium for new vehicles turn to secondary market for used cars. 


Finding the right price is hard


Unlike new cars where prices are guided by MSRP, used cars vary a lot in prices, making it extremely difficult to determine a fair price for a deal. The process can be long and gruesome for many.

                   



That's why we wanted to develop a tool, with the help of Machine Learning algorithms, to help potential buyers determine, based on historical data, the right price to pay for their desired used cars.


Features

We have developed tools that not only allow users to explore historical data, but also make predictions on the specific car they want.

EXPLORATION

Freely explore and search through our database with over 300k+ entries of data collected from Craigslist.org.

MODIFY DATA

Add your own data to the dataset and store it in the cloud.

PREDICTION

Input your parameters and obtain instant result based on our Machine Learning Algorithms.
